Get started


Homepage
Enter the group's name to access the vote page.
Recent inputs are saved on the device.
Vote page
Votes and comments are two separate entities. One can be used regardless of the other.
Votes are weighted from 1 to 5. If there's any mistake in your vote, revote and your last one will be considered.
For the comments, press enter to submit it.
Chart page
The default period is "global". Other statistics such as "average mood" will show up in the other periods.
Period's timeframe and comments are sorted by most recent.
